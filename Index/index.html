Heroes of Our Planet

Protecting our planet is vital for everyone now and in the future. Earth gives us things we need to live, like fresh air, clean water, and food. When we take care of nature, we keep different plants and animals safe, which is important for a strong and healthy environment.

EARTHDAY 2024

For Earth Day 2024 on April 22nd, EARTHDAY.ORG is unwavering in our commitment to end plastics for the sake of human and planetary health, demanding a 60% reduction in the production of ALL plastics by 2040.



GOALS

'Heroes of Our Planet' is in line with the 5 Sustainable Development Goals created by the United Nations in 2015. The 5 goals we chose are related to maintaining our environment so that it remains clean and comfortable.
