Planting Trees Online with Ecosia Search Engine




Ecosia is a search engine that plants tree every time you use the search engine.
You might ask, what is Ecosia search engine Why would I use it as my primary search engine?


Human actions have significantly impacted trees in various ways throughout history.
• Cutting down trees for various purposes, such as construction, paper production, and energy, has led to significant deforestation of 15 billion trees.
• Clearing forests for agriculture, urban development, and infrastructure has further contributed to deforestation.


These actions had significant consequences for our environment and ecosystems.
• When forests are cleared, countless species lose their homes. Many plants, animals, and insects rely on specific tree species for survival.
• Trees absorb carbon dioxide (CO₂) during photosynthesis, acting as a natural carbon sink. Deforestation releases stored carbon back into the atmosphere, contributing to global warming and also mitigating climate change.
• Trees can improve water quality, when they're gone, water can be easily poluted.
Source : Time


This is when Ecosia search engine came in clutch for our planet.
Ecosia was launched on 7 December 2009 with UN climate talks in Copenhagen. Ecosiahas supported various tree-planting programs. At launch, Ecosia search engine provided a combination of search results from Yahoo! and Microsoft Bing.
Ecosia uses 80% of its profit from advertising revenue to support tree-plantin projects. By April 2024, Ecosia planted almost 205 million of trees by the Ecosia community.
